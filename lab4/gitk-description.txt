The top left window shows us the structure of the repository and the different
branches in the repository. It shows us where the HEAD and master branch are
as well as the commits made on them.

The window in the upper middle column shows the name and email address of the
person who made the commit.

The window in the upper right hand corner shows us when the commit was made.

Below that shows a window in which information about the commit is shown.
This includes the name and email of the person who made the commit, the time
stamp in which the commit was made and parent and child commits.

The window below the one described above shows changes made to the repository
in a diff format.

The window to the right of the one described above shows files that were
changed or modified from the commit
